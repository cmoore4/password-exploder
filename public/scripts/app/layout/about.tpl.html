Password Exploder allows you to share passwords with unguessable URLs. Tell it when to delete the password (for instance: after 10 minutes, or after it has been viewed 3 times) and the password is deleted without a trace from the server at that time.

Yeah, but, why?

We often found ourselves needing to share passwords with clients, but didn't want to send via email or have to spell out a long random password over the phone. This method is not 100% secure, but it's pretty effective.

There are a ton of ways to share passwords securely, and this works frictionlessly for us for non-mission-critical sharing.
